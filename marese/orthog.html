Orthography and Phonology

 

Sound Character Description
/ʘ/ Q A lip-smack, like a kiss but with no puckering.
/m̩/ M A brief closed-mouth hum.
/ǀ/ C A "tsk!" sound.
/ǁ/ G Like C above, but instead of the tip of your tongue on your front teeth, it's the sides of your tongue on your molars.
/!/ K A loud tongue click.
/ʃ̩/ S A "shush".
/f̩/ F A "fff" sound.
/ç̩/ H A hiss, like a cat.
/t͡s̩/ T A "tss" sound, like a cymbal.
/ɚ̰/ R A creaky "rrr" sound.
