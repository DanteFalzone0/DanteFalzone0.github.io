Basic Vocab and Grammar

 

Word order is OSV (object-subject-verb), though the line between object and subject can be fuzzy at best.

Word Meanings
QM I/me; we/us
QK you
QC they/them
QH someone/anyone
MSF water
GRK to grok; to reach Understanding with; to master
KC to say
SK to speak with; to converse with
HTR headspace; (mind)set; wherever a mind resides
MM no; not; don't
SFS to surrender/yield to; to accept; to trust in
MQS God; Mother Nature; the Universe; the universal Presence

Example sentence: "MQS QM SFS." ("I yield to God" or "I accept God's will").
