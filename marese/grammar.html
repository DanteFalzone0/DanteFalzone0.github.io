Grammar, cont. from basic vocab

 

There are several words used as particles, or as parts of larger words. These can be separated by spaces or by apostrophes. Examples are at the bottom.

Word Meanings
TG the one who is called; the one whose name is
TR he/she/they who; that which
KT without; lacking; (as a verb) to lack
FH does (despite sth.)
QCM to be (at the moment; like Spanish "estar")
QCK to Be (eternally; like Spanish "ser")
RT to go to; to arrive at

Examples

TG'QQCQK TR'KT'KC'FC'QH'SK QCK.

{the-one-named "QQCQK"} {the-one-who without to-say/talk does someone/anyone converse-with} to-Be

"The one named QQCQK is the one who converses without opening their mouth."
